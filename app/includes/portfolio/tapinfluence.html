2013-2014 UI/UX, Design, Development

Project Description

Combining the leading marketplace of talented and influential content creators with end-to-end workflow, TapInfluence streamlines comprehensive influencer marketing.

As the only UX/UI Designer at TapInfluence, I was responsible for maintaining existing design and affordance patterns across the application. The design was defined by a previous contractor, therefore the majority of my design work for the platform was adapted to that defined style. I have, however, included a couple of concept designs that illustrate the direction I was hoping to take the overall design.

Technologies Used

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, TapInfluence Platform, HTML, CSS, some Javascript
