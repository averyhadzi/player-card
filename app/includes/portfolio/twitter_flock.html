Project Description

The Twitter Flock microsite was a centralized portal for Twitter's worldwide developer conference tour. The site was updated to include post-event photos and presentation slides in five different languages. The challenge here was to be able to view and download .pdf documents of the slides. If a document in a specific language was not yet available, the option would be locked.

This microsite won the 2015 W3 Gold Award in September 2015 by the Academy of Interactive and Visual Arts. Click here to view the award.
